Conduct & Policy

Building Rules & Conduct

These rules apply to all residents and visitors of SS Serengeti. Please familiarise yourself with them. Violations may result in a formal warning or further action by management.

🔊

Noise & Quiet Hours

  • Quiet hours are from 22:00 to 07:00 on weekdays.
  • Quiet hours are from 23:00 to 09:00 on weekends and public holidays.
  • Loud music, parties, or disturbances that affect neighbours are not permitted.
  • Visitors must also adhere to quiet hours.
  • Repeated offences will result in a formal warning and possible eviction.
🚗

Parking Rules

  • Only park in your allocated parking bay.
  • Do not park in visitor bays overnight.
  • Vehicles must be roadworthy and display a valid licence disc.
  • No repairs, panel beating, or oil changes in the parking area.
  • Washing of vehicles is only permitted in designated areas.
🧺

Communal Areas

  • Keep passages, stairwells, and lobbies clean and clear at all times.
  • No items (bicycles, furniture, boxes) may be stored in common areas.
  • Smoking is only permitted in designated outdoor areas.
  • No braais or open flames on balconies or in common areas.
  • Report any damage to communal areas to management immediately.
🦴

Pets

  • Pets require prior written approval from management.
  • Pets must be on a leash in all common areas.
  • Owners are responsible for cleaning up after their pets.
  • Pets that cause a nuisance (noise, aggression) may be removed.
  • No pets are permitted in the lifts.
🚪

Security & Access

  • Do not allow access to unknown persons through the gate or intercom.
  • Gate remotes are not to be shared with non-residents.
  • Report any lost remotes or keys to security immediately.
  • Do not prop doors or the gate open.
  • Security guards are on duty 24/7 — report all concerns directly to them.
🚮

Refuse & Waste

  • Use the designated refuse bins on your floor.
  • Refuse collection is on Tuesdays and Fridays.
  • Do not leave bags outside your unit door.
  • Bulky items must be arranged for removal with management.
  • Recyclables should be separated where possible.
🔨

Alterations & Maintenance

  • No structural alterations without written approval from management.
  • Nails and hooks in walls must not exceed reasonable use.
  • Report all maintenance issues via the Maintenance Request page.
  • Do not attempt to repair electrical or plumbing issues yourself.
  • Units must be kept in a reasonable state of cleanliness.
👥

Visitors & Guests

  • Residents are responsible for the conduct of their visitors.
  • Guests may not stay longer than 7 consecutive nights without written notice.
  • Long-term guests must be formally added to the lease.
  • All visitors must be registered with security at the main gate.

Power Outages

Loadshedding Information

South Africa experiences scheduled power outages known as loadshedding. Use the resources below to stay informed and plan ahead.

📱

EskomSePush App

The most reliable tool for checking your exact loadshedding schedule. Available on Android and iOS. Search "EskomSePush" in your app store.

Recommended
🌐

Eskom Website

Check the official Eskom website for current stage announcements and scheduled outage information for your municipality.

Visit Eskom Website
🏘

Our Area & Stage Info

SS Serengeti is located in Cape Town, Western Cape. Our municipality's schedule applies. Management will post notices here and on the Notices board for planned outages that affect our building.

🔬

Building During Loadshedding

  • Common area lighting has limited battery backup.
  • The lift does not operate during loadshedding.
  • The electric gate switches to manual operation — security will be present.
  • Your gate remote will still work during Stage 1–4.
  • Keep a torch and power bank handy.
